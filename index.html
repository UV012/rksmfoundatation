12AB Registration

The Foundation is registered under Section 12AB of the Income Tax Act, 1961, which recognises us as a charitable organisation and exempts our income from tax when applied towards our charitable objects. This registration is renewed periodically and is a prerequisite for holding valid 80G status.

🧾

80G Tax Benefit

Donations made to the Foundation may qualify for a deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Eligible donors can claim this deduction while filing their income tax returns using the donation receipt and our 80G registration details. Please consult your tax advisor to confirm eligibility and the applicable deduction limit.
