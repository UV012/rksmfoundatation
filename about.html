Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Service

The Ram Kinkar Shahi Memorial Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation licensed under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. We are dedicated to the welfare of underprivileged communities across Bihar and beyond — carrying forward the compassionate vision of the man in whose memory we were founded.

Our work spans education, healthcare, disaster relief, women's empowerment, skill development, and sports — always guided by the belief that every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and care regardless of caste, creed, gender, or region.
