What We Do

Our Core Objectives

Eight pillars of action that define how we serve our communities every day.

🤝

Charitable Welfare

Promoting charitable activities for the poor, needy, and underprivileged without discrimination of caste, creed, religion, gender, or region.

🏠

Basic Necessities

Providing food, clothing, shelter, healthcare, and education to economically weaker sections of society.

📚

Education Support

Granting scholarships, financial assistance, books, uniforms, and other educational support to deserving students.

🏥

Healthcare & Medical Aid

Organising medical camps, health awareness programs, blood donation camps, and providing medical aid and medicines to the needy.

🌊

Disaster Relief

Undertaking relief, rehabilitation, and support activities during natural calamities, pandemics, disasters, and other emergencies.

👩‍👧

Women & Community Uplift

Promoting welfare programs for women, children, youth, and senior citizens for their social and economic upliftment.

🔧

Skill Development

Undertaking vocational training and livelihood enhancement programs for sustainable development of communities.

⚽

Sports & Physical Education

Promoting sports, yoga, and athletics among children and youth from weaker sections through training, tournaments, and equipment support.
