Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Ram Kinkar Shahi Memorial Foundation ("the Foundation", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and individuals who interact with us, including donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Contact details you provide voluntarily, such as name, email address, and phone number (e.g. via our Contact form)
  • Donation-related information, if and when online donation facilities are made available
  • Technical information such as browser type and general usage data, for improving our website

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our Contact form
  • To process and acknowledge donations, and issue donation receipts where applicable
  • To communicate updates about our programs and activities, where you have consented to receive them
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including those relating to our 12AB and 80G registrations under the Income Tax Act, 1961

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information with government or regulatory authorities where required by law, or with service providers who assist us in operating our website and programs, under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the personal information we hold from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, to request corrections, or to ask that we stop contacting you.

6. Children's Privacy

While our programs may benefit children, we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through this website without appropriate consent from a parent or guardian.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
